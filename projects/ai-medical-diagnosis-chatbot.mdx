## Project: AI Medical Diagnosis Chatbot

### Description
In this project, you will develop an AI chatbot that assists healthcare professionals in diagnosing medical conditions based on patient symptoms and medical history. This project will help you understand how to use AI for medical applications and decision support.

### Project Prompt
- Develop an AI chatbot that can understand patient symptoms and medical history to provide probable diagnoses and recommendations.
- Use NLP to process patient inputs and generate medical insights.
- Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and data privacy standards.
- Create a user-friendly interface for healthcare professionals to interact with the chatbot.

### Getting Started
1. Choose a suitable NLP model for processing medical data (e.g., BioBERT).
2. Set up a backend service to handle medical data processing and diagnosis generation.
3. Develop the frontend interface for healthcare professional interaction.
4. Implement features for symptom checking, medical history analysis, and diagnosis generation.
5. Test the chatbot with various medical scenarios to ensure accuracy and compliance.

### Deliverable
An AI medical diagnosis chatbot that assists healthcare professionals by providing probable diagnoses and recommendations based on patient symptoms and medical history, with a user-friendly interface for interaction.
